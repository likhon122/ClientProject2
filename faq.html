General

MOCAT License Number
MOCAT License Number

If you decided to take a local tour, please ensure that the tour company / travel agency has this license. This license is issued by Ministry of Culture, Arts and Tourism of Malaysia, to control operation & prove legitimacy of one company.

There are a lot of inbound tour companies in Sabah, but some of them are illegal. If you choose a local tour with cheaper price but they don’t have this license, be aware that it maybe a trap, or they will have some hidden cost that you never know before you get on their car.

The differences between a bad and a good tour company are:
  • Tour guides training - A qualified tour guide has to take & passed the examination which held by government.
  • Professional - They will take care of everyone even there are a lot of visitors.
  • Number of tour buses - Most of the tour companies don’t have their own van/buses. Sometimes they will cancel the itinerary or pass to other company if number of visitors too little.
  • What’s included - Well listed terms so that you know the inclusive and exclusive items.

Tour

What if my hotel / accommodation is not listed?

You can check with us if tourist van can pick you up from your hotel / accommodation.

Could I request a Chinese speaking tour guide?

Normally, the tour guide is English speaking, because there are only a few Chinese speaking tour guides in Sabah.

But if your group has 6 peoples (adults & children) and above, you may request a Chinese speaking (Mandarin / Cantonese) tour guide. During low season, we could even try to arrange a Private Tour for you with private tour guide, private van and no others tourists.

Additionally, if the tour has noted that "you may request a Chinese speaking tour guide", feel free to indicate your request when you book no matter how many persons you have, but we can’t guarantee for the arrangement.

How do I keep contact with you upon arrived?

Once your booking has confirmed, we will send you a Reservation Receipt with tour company contact number, as well as the contact number of Sabah Booking. You are welcome to contact us whenever you need, otherwise contact & reconfirm is not necessary.

All you need to do is waiting the tour guide at hotel lobby to pickup you on travelling day. If some situation occurs like tour has been cancelled due to bad weather, tour company will contact and inform you.

Any additional charges for private tour?

In most case, if you have 7 to 10 people, private tour could be arranged with no additional charge. However, you still have to share the boat with others during sea / river transfer.

What if I have not yet booked a hotel?

Simply tick "To be confirmed / Inform later" checkbox upon booking. Once your hotel arrangements are finalized, you can login "My Booking" to update pickup location anytime.
