Description

Welcome to our sunset Party Boat Sunset cruise in Kota Kinabalu! Prepare for an exciting evening filled with breathtaking views, unlimited drinks, and fun fishing activities. The party boat sunset cruise is the ideal way to unwind after a long day of exploring Kota Kinabalu, 

When you step onto our boat, you will be greeted by our friendly crew, who will attend to all of your needs throughout the journey. With a fully stocked bar on board, you can enjoy unlimited drinks while admiring the picturesque sunset over the South China Sea. And if you 're not into fishing, don 't worry! Simply sit back, relax, and enjoy the company of your friends and loved ones while soaking in the beautiful surroundings. For fishing avids or interested in fishing can experience  fun fishing activities  to try having a shot at catching some fresh fish. Our experienced crew will provide you with all of the necessary equipment and guidance to ensure a successful catch.

So why wait? Book your spot on our party boat sunset cruise now and get ready for a memorable evening filled with free-flow drinks, fun fishing, and breathtaking views.  Whether you 're a solo traveller, a couple, or a group of friends, we guarantee you 'll have an unforgettable time on board.

How to Pay

Pay on travelling day

No advance payment is required, simply pay cash in Ringgit-Malaysia to tour guide. However, you still need pay in full for any cancellation made 1 day prior to tour date.

Pay Online

Accep Visa and MasterCard credit card, Malaysian bank transfer, Paypal, FPX, Alipay. 2.3% surcharge apply. Please be noted that 2.3% processing fee is non-refundable in case there is cancellation 3 days after the payment made.

Bank Transfer

Please request bank staff to calculate equivalent amount in USD before Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) to Malaysia. It takes 3 days to reach us. No 2.3% surcharge but you have to bear both side banks charges. The balances will be paid to either side as the case may be.
