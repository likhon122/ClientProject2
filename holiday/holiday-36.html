Description

Mataking

Mataking Island is made up of the "Kecil"(Small island) and the "Besar"(Big island), these islands are connected through a sandbar making visitors have a unique experience of feeling like walking on water. 

The journey from Semporna to Mataking takes about 45 minutes by boat.

With its sparkling white sand, mesmerizing blue water and top-class resort making Mataking is the best place for a getaway. Divers can gear up and explore the Eel Garden, Coral Garden, Nudibranch Garden, Turtle Playground, Lobster Lair, and Hump Head Point.

The island is also known for the newlyweds spending their honeymoon since it's a romantic place to spend with their loved ones.

 

Timba Timba Island

With its long white sandy beach and photogenic landscape, the island attracts visitors looking forward to a picture-perfect vacation.

Every corner of the island is an opportunity to pose and snap a memorable picture. You can rest at the gazebo, lounge on the hammock, or find your sunbathing spot anywhere on the island. The alluring blue hues surrounding the island make it irresistible to swim on it.

 

Pom Pom Island

Pom Pom Island is a small coral reef island in the Celebs Sea. The island is 2.3km in circumference at the high tide line with fringing coral reefs 4km long.

The island is rich with many rare and uncommon species. The region is a part of the coral triangle which has 500-plus coral species.

Pom Pom Island is the best place for you to visit and spend some time relaxing or sunbathing. If you wish to see an amazing view to see how the little baby sea turtles get out from their nest, you can come around the month of May and August, since this is the time when sea turtles nest on the island.
