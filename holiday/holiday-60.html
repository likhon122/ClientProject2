Description

Tucked away in the middle of the South China Sea is a secret paradise familiar only to a handful of savvy locals. A 20 minutes boat ride from Kota Kinabalu will get you to the sea-horse shaped island, known by the locals as Dinawan Island. It is the ultimate island getaway experience: pristine beaches, crystal clear water and unexplored forests.

This private island offers everything one could wish for: untouched nature, eco-friendly facilities and, above all... the luxury of peace. Whether you're here for the sun or for the fun, let us cater to all your envies. Spend your day lazed under a coconut tree, watching the world goes by... or go snorkeling and kayaking. Dinawan island is a hidden jewel for you to enjoy.

Pay on travelling day

No advance payment is required, simply pay cash in Ringgit-Malaysia to tour guide. However, you still need pay in full for any cancellation made 1 day prior to tour date.

Pay Online

Accep Visa and MasterCard credit card, Malaysian bank transfer, Paypal, FPX, Alipay. 2.3% surcharge apply. Please be noted that 2.3% processing fee is non-refundable in case there is cancellation 3 days after the payment made.

Bank Transfer

Please request bank staff to calculate equivalent amount in USD before Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) to Malaysia. It takes 3 days to reach us. No 2.3% surcharge but you have to bear both side banks charges. The balances will be paid to either side as the case may be.
