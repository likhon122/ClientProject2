Kota Kinabalu Airport to City

  • Book Now

Highlight

  • Private transfer, not sharing with others
  • Point to Point Transfer
  • Tour guide will be provided if more than 7 passengers

Transport Type

Transfer Service (One Way)

Description

One way transfer to destination, no stopover at any point unless agreed upon booking.

Midnight transfer period with 50% surcharge: 10pm till 7am

The driver will meet you at arrival hall with a signage.

Heading to: Kota Kinabalu city

It takes about 20 minutes from Kota Kinabalu International Airport to the city.

MYR 20 extra charge will be applied to every 15 minutes stopover on the way to destination. Please notify us upon booking, request on the spot will not be entertained.

 

 

 

How to Pay

Pay upon transfer

No advance payment is required, simply pay cash in Ringgit-Malaysia to driver. However, you still need pay in full for any cancellation made after confirmation.

Pay Online

Accep Visa and MasterCard credit card, Malaysian bank transfer, Paypal, FPX, Alipay. 2.3% surcharge apply. Please be noted that 2.3% processing fee is non-refundable in case there is cancellation 3 days after the payment made.

Bank Transfer

Please request bank staff to calculate equivalent amount in USD before Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) to Malaysia. It takes 3 days to reach us. No 2.3% surcharge but you have to bear both side banks charges. The balances will be paid to either side as the case may be.

Additional information

  • Service location: Kota Kinabalu

  • Recommend to book: 1 month(s) in advance

  • To book at least: 3 day(s) in advance

  • Cancellation & Refund Policy: Not refunable for any cancellation made.
